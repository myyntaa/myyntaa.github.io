This privacy policy explains how Myntaa collects, uses, and protects the personal information of users of our mobile application Bitcoin Switch.




What information do we collect from users?


We collect the following information from users:

- Email and password: This information is collected so that users can create an account and log in to their account.

- Device ID: This information is collected to prevent cheating or hacking and to ban any devices associated with such activities.


How is the collected data used?


The collected data (email, password, device ID) is only used to allow users to create an account and log in to their account. The device ID is also used to ban any devices associated with cheating or hacking.


Does our app share or sell user data to third parties?


No, we do not share or sell user data to third parties. Our app does not use any third-party packages or SDKs.


How is the data stored and protected?


User data is safely stored and encrypted inside namecheap.com hosting services. We take reasonable measures to protect the data against any unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.


What are the users' rights regarding their data?


Users do not have the ability to update their email or password after signing up. However, if a user wants to delete their data, they can submit a request directly via email to myyntaa@gmail.com.


Changes to this privacy policy


We reserve the right to update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on our website and updating the "last updated" date at the top of the policy.


Contact us


If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or our use of your personal information, please contact us at myyntaa@gmail.com.
